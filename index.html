About Hacktoberfest

Hacktoberfest is an annual event held in October to encourage open-source contributions from around the world. It’s a celebration of everything open-source and the power of the community. Whether you're a developer, student learning to code, event host, or company of any size, you can help drive the growth of open-source and make positive contributions to an ever-growing community.

How to Participate

  • Step 1: Register on the official Hacktoberfest website.
  • Step 2: Contribute to open-source projects on GitHub.
  • Step 3: Make 4 valid pull requests between October 1st and October 31st.
  • Step 4: Earn a limited edition Hacktoberfest T-shirt or plant a tree as a reward.
